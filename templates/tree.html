I'm a graphic designer and Apple enthusiast currently at Stanford. I started using Apple products after pitching my parents on a family Mac back in 2006 and I haven't looked back since. | twitter.com/viraj_bindra
15 years, 11 products with Apple
1998
iMac

I remember my brother and I used to fight over the iMac to play MathBlaster and ClueFinders (the educational but fun games our parents tricked us into playing). I loved staring through the see through back wondering how things worked.

2001
Powerbook G4

When I got this, no one else had it. Apple was still something weird and different. I loved this Powerbook until someone sat on it in an airplane and broke the plasma screen. We couldn't afford to fix it so I had to go back to my Toshiba.
